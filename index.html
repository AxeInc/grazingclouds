A Tribute To The Poet

This site is an ovation to the late poet from his wife, children, grand children, daughters-in-law and son-in-law who love him ever-so dearly. We hope to keep his legacy alive by setting up this website, containing every last poem from his book of poems, Grazing Clouds. He also wrote a collection of Malayalam poems, titled,"Manadan Kavithakal", or "Manadan Poems". Manadath, being the poet's family name.

Many A Mickle Makes A Muckle

This section of the site is not to be confused the book, Grazing Clouds. Many A Mickle Makes A Muckle is a very distinct collection consisting of accolades, newspaper and magazine articles featuring Mr. Khader. This collection is maintained by Mr. Abdul's wife, Mrs. Meherunissa. These extravagant articles are written in both Malayalam and English. Enjoy this epic collection of articles.
