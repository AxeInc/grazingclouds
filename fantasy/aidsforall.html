Aids For All

We have a terrible virus,
The plague
That knows
No boundaries.

Worldwide,
Thousand are infected each day,
While their survival is almost bleak.

Among those who mate,
Million develop AIDS.

Most of them
Will soon die,
Leaving heavy imprints
On their beloved ones.

No vaccine or cure
Yet exist,
While health care
Can only check
The blasting typhoon
Sweeping homes world-wide.

Our lust
Knew no bounds,
As free sex
Flared our emotions,
When His wrath
Came battering down,
Giving us no refuge.

While the Lord commanded
Against adultery,
Sodomy soon became
An affair
To remember.

He thundered in anger-
"Let all this rot melt
To stop the burning outrage"

As the mating
Became open
Than the eating,
His dictum worked.

Men and Women
All over the world
Are dying for their passion !

So,
Innocents die
In the coss-fire,
As blood
Is thicker than water.

Experts concur:
None will die
Of this retro-virus,
As long you live
A normal life,
Shaking hands, hugging
Kissing gently,
Eating food,
And drinking water
The Arab way.

Mosquitoes
Never transmit the virus,
As they long
For adventure.

With the looming risk factor,
You and me
Are potential victims
By our own actions
And the innocent doings
Of even those
Who profess to treat us
In an emergency

Every storm
Finally calms
Before long.
Let us then
Endure the fury,
Until we chain
The mad frenzy,
Facing the world
With empathy.

Who knows
God has chosen
This deadly curse,
Just to topple
The modem ways
Of curbing our breeds,
As we put a stop
To new inmates
Here on earth
By unholy means.

He alone can assure
The survival of human race,
As we can never stop
What He has destined,
For He plans only to execute !
