About Mr. A. Khader

The Prophesy

The ancient kings believed their seers, while,
Modem leaders heed to their fortune-tellers.

When Manu was an unknown camp follower,
A little known palmist, Rama Sastry
Of Kondanhalli Village forecast
That he would rule India
With authority,
Befitting kings of the ancient yore.

Shatri predicted
His spendid rise,
With changing times;
Using fuzzy logics,
While looking beyond stars
That move here and there;
Matching his wits
With the science of numbers;
To make his forecast
Perfect,
Like a print-out
From a super computer.

All went well, and the great Manu
Became the supreme leader of Bharath;
Consulting his Godmen, now and then,
Only to confirm the future predictions,
Some of which made him so uneasy.
The date and the time of his death
Bothered him greatly while his heart
Started ticking faster, as the appointed day
Approached, sooner than expected.
He would die
Early in the morning,
On his 55th birthday
In the Rajadhani,
After a stroke,
On the dawn of the fateful day,
In the year of his first term in office.

Red alert was sounded,
As the Prime Minister
Was confined to five-star luxury
In the make-shift VIP ward,
Befitting a monarch about to die.

At the stroke of dawn,
The Regency and the entire nation
Was alive to the impending tale,
As the electronic media
Relayed live commentories
Full of medical info
Released by a panel of experts,
While the PM stay put
Meshed in an array of wires
Tagged to the monitors,
Displaying on the screen
Every beat of his heart.

The appointed hour came.
The seconds ticked slowly,
As the PM's heart
Went racing fast.

Someone murmured:
"The king is dead
Long live the king",
As his companion corrected-
It is our PM,
For all our kings are dead and gone.

Nothing ever happened,
As the nation faced the ordeal
Tense with relief,
As the leader passed the test
Beyond the prophesy.

The country sighed,
when the leader Got up from his induced sleep,
And his would be
death-bed In the ICU
Inside the PM's residence;
For him to complete his second term
As the Prime Minister
In the land of occult frenzy.

The foretellers
Rushed to the Rajadhani
To tell
Their leader
How the horoscope
Was Wrongly decoded,
As his dotting parents
Had given vital data,
Based on lunar calendar.

Whatever be the flaw,
Our leader is ticking strong,
While all the predictions
Must now be recast,
Knowing his present status;
As Manu is still an enigma
To the teeming masses ! !
