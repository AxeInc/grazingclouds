The Crowning Court

The right to command
Is an authority
Even in a democracy.

For all,
The power trickling
In the hierarchy
Drags,
Until challenged
By a lesser mortal !
The Minister
May tell the police -
Be cool
To his collegues misdeeds.
While the Inspector misfires,
He prostrates before the court
For having flouted the law.

None is immune
From the reigns
Except the apex court;
Before whom we stand
In mute silence;
As it decrees against us.

In utter desperation,
We throw-up our arms
In the hallowed chamber
