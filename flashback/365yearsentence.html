A 365 - Year Sentence

Jerry had no conscience
No qualm whatsoever
To betray his country
Accepting the millions he received
From the powers in Kremlin
On whom America had zeroed
Its deadliest missiles
With total precision
Clocked in utmost secrecy
When the all knowing super-spy
Jerry Whitworth sold all
The classified information to the KGB
To decipher over a million
U.S. Navy messages marked top-secret
At a price he could demand
Including the super-secret codes
Used by the National Command Authority
The name given to the White House
Office of the Supreme Commander
The President of United States of America
Whereby throwing to shambles the complete
Network of secret communications far and wide
Perfected by the Americans for long
As the Kremlin totally exposed the CIA
With the help of the principal agent
Jerry, now, completely in their pocket
That even new codes and
Coded commands can never
Protect the Americans in a crisis
Paralysing their entire defence operations
Within minutes of any offensive
As a traitor extraordinary Jerry knew
Too much and conveyed all
To the Russians before he was caught
Only to be indicted by the San Francisco
Federal District Court later
When the Judge John Vukasin
Chose to award
365 years in prison
Stipulating parole after 60 years
When Jerry would be 107 years old
In the year 2046 A.D.
While U.S. Attorney
Joseph Russionello in a memorandum
To the federal court affirmed
That the Government would not seek
Death penalty in this case
As the jam-packed court screamed
Why not death penalty
On hearing the unusual sentence
The learned Attorney confessed
Federal law does not
Provide capital punishment
For peace time espionage!


* A one line poem based on historic events as it happened in the
year 1986. The events and characters are real.
