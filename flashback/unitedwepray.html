About Mr. A. Khader

United We Pray

Holy recitals from the temples,
The shrill call of the muezzin,
Chiming church bells;
All we hear
Loud and clear;
Vibrating the morning air.

Whatever be our faith,
The Lord knows our deeds,
Besides our transient mind.

The style we pray
Well differ,
Though the essence
Is much the same.

Sublime be our prayers
Before the Supreme,
As we bow down
Seeking many a blessing;
Calling Him subtle names.

While we move closer
To a place of worship
In daylight,
Or during midnight hour,
May our hearts hum
Sacred hymns
In praise of God,
As we stand near
A church, a temple or a mosque;
Each a House of our Lord !!
